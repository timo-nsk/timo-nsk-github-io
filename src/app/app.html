That's me

My name is Timo Neske. I am a computer science student at the Heinrich Heine Universität in Düsseldorf.

I’m a passionate software developer specializing in backend systems, microservice architectures, and data automation. My work focuses on building scalable solutions with the Spring Boot Framework, and RESTful communication between distributed services. I also use Docker for orchestration and Angular for clean, responsive frontends.

I'm a relaxed person who is very structured and organized with a good sense of humor and I have a keen interest for nerdy topics in various fields. I also love playing Badminton and sometimes you can see me cooking like Jamie Oliver (not as good as him).
